## Notes

All books in this collection are from Project Gutenberg and are in the public domain in the United States.

### Key Intellectual Connections:

**Taleb's Core Influences:**
- **Stoicism** (especially Seneca): Practical wisdom, robustness
- **Skeptics** (Montaigne, Hume): Limits of knowledge
- **Nietzsche**: Critique of rationalism
- **Darwin**: Evolution as antifragile process

**Vassar's Core Influences:**
- **Yudkowsky**: Rationality, AI safety
- **Russell**: Logic and philosophy
- **William James**: Psychology and pragmatism
- **Enlightenment philosophers**: Epistemology

**Gupta's Core Influences:**
- **Systems thinkers**: Fuller, appropriate technology
- **Political philosophers**: Paine, Locke
- **Darwin**: Evolutionary resilience
- **Newton**: Scientific foundations
